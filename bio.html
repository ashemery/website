SUMMARY OF QUALIFICATIONS

Ali Hadi is a distinguished Senior Cybersecurity Specialist with an impressive 14+ years in the Information Technology sector. Currently, he leads as the Research Director at the Leahy Center for Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity, and holds a prominent role as a full-time professor and program director at Champlain College's Computer and Digital Forensics Program in the USA. Ali's academic credentials are extensive, including a PhD and MSc in Computer Information Systems, complemented by a BSc in Computer Science. His professional journey is marked by the acquisition of over 20 professional certifications. In the realm of cybersecurity, Ali is a highly sought-after consultant, specializing in digital forensics, incident response, adversary simulation, and malware analysis. His expertise extends to being an established author, speaker, and freelance instructor, where he has imparted technical training to various government and private entities, enhancing their capabilities in critical cybersecurity areas. Ali's influence in the digital forensics community is significant. He is dedicated to advancing the field through his commitment to forensics education and research, continually contributing to the growth and development of this vital sector. His work not only reflects his deep expertise but also his passion for nurturing and disseminating knowledge in cybersecurity and digital forensics.
