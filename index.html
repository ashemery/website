Teaching

As an experienced technical instructor for the Linux Operating System, I have developed a teaching philosophy that emphasizes the importance of student participation in achieving success. From my experience lecturing for various companies and organizations, I have found that the most effective classes are those in which students are actively engaged in the learning process.

To encourage classroom participation, I meticulously organize each lecture to provide a clear framework for students to follow. This framework helps students understand the course material better and feel more comfortable asking questions about sections they do not understand. In addition, I believe in fostering a more participatory classroom environment by asking individual students thought-provoking questions that encourage discussion and active participation.

My ultimate goal is to help my students not just learn, but also retain the knowledge and skills that will serve them well beyond the classroom. By engaging them in the learning process and creating a supportive learning environment, I hope to instill in them a lifelong love of learning and a desire to continue exploring the subjects they are studying.
